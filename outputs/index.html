What is Apex Trade Lab?

A paper trading simulation — a virtual portfolio trading TQQQ and SQQQ based on 7 algorithmic strategies. No real money. One decision per day, 10 minutes before market close.

What is paper trading?

Simulated trading with virtual money. A flight simulator for trading.

The Signal Card at the top shows what the system is doing and why. The equity curve tracks performance vs QQQ. The regime indicator shows bull or bear market status.

Inside the System — 7 Strategies

Each strategy has different entry conditions, goals, and risk profiles. They combine for a smoother equity curve.

🟢 Momentum LongTQQQ
When: Uptrend
Entry: NDX breaks above upper Bollinger Band + above SMA(20).
Goal: Ride strong upward momentum.
Risk: Stops if NDX closes below SMA(20).
Example: In a strong bull, NDX pushes above the upper BB. System goes heavily long.
🟢 MR Long 1 (SMA20 Pullback)TQQQ
When: Uptrend
Entry: NDX pulls back to within 1% of SMA(20).
Goal: Buy dips in uptrends.
Risk: Exits if NDX extends 3%+ above SMA(20).
Example: After a 2-3% pullback in a bull market, the system buys expecting a bounce.
🟢 MR Long 2 (Deep Pullback)TQQQ
When: Uptrend
Entry: NDX drops below lower Bollinger Band but above SMA(250).
Goal: Buy deep dips aggressively.
Risk: Exits above middle BB.
Example: A sharp 5%+ pullback in uptrend — the system sees a sale.
🟢 MR Long 3 (SMA250 Bounce)TQQQ
When: Near SMA(250)
Entry: NDX within 3% of SMA(250) + bullish candle.
Goal: Catch bounces at the critical SMA(250) support.
Risk: Exits if extended 5%+ above SMA(20).
Example: NDX tests its 250-day average and bounces.
🔴 MR Short (Overextension)SQQQ
When: Uptrend (extended)
Entry: NDX 4%+ above SMA(20) AND above upper BB.
Goal: Fade extreme overextension.
Risk: Exits when NDX reverts to SMA(20).
Example: After a massive rally, the system takes a small contrarian short.
🔴 Momentum Short 1 (Breakdown)SQQQ
When: Downtrend
Entry: NDX closes below SMA(250) + below lower BB.
Goal: Ride the downtrend. Size scales with depth.
Risk: Exits when NDX reclaims SMA(250).
Example: NDX breaks its 250-day average — major bearish signal.
🔴 Momentum Short 2 (Failed Bounce)SQQQ
When: Downtrend
Entry: NDX bounced to SMA(20) then closed below it.
Goal: Short bull traps in bear markets.
Risk: Exits if above SMA(20) for 3 days.
Example: In a downtrend, a rally that fails is a classic short entry.
